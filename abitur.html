Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum GPA requirement?

GPA requirements vary by program. Generally, we require a minimum 2.8 GPA, but competitive programs may require higher.

Can I apply to multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply to up to 3 programs, but you must submit separate applications for each.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer various scholarships, grants, and work-study programs. Complete the FAFSA to be considered.

What if I miss the application deadline?

Late applications may be considered on a case-by-case basis, but we strongly encourage submitting before the deadline.
